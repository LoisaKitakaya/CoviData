About


CoviData is a web application that shows the user Covid-19 statistics; filtering the data into the following categories:
World (general world statistics).
Continent (every country's data within it's parent continent).
CoviData is however not limited to only showing Covid-19 statistics. One can also find news on the virus and/or related topics.
